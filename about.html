Hi, My name is Muhammad Osama and I am an aspiring Data Analyst / Data Scientist with a passion for storytelling. I hold a Master's degree in Applied Data Analytics from Boston University.

Since I was young, I have always enjoyed to solve puzzles. So that is how I look at big data sets: to me it is one big puzzle I want to solve. Finding patterns nobody else sees is the challenge to me.

My skills include data analysis, visualization, and machine learning. I have developed a strong acumen for problem solving, and I enjoy an occasional challenge. I often work on end-to-end data science projects that usually begin from collecting data from third party sources and end with delivering business insight in the form of customer segments.
